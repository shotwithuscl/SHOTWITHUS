I'm Sebastián Cerpa, a professional photographer and technical producer with over 10 years of experience in advertising photography. I started as a camera assistant and digi tech, working alongside dozens of top-level advertising photographers — both local and international. That hands-on experience across every position is what allows me to anticipate every need before it becomes a problem.

I created Shot With Us to be the local extension of photographers, agencies, and international production companies coming to shoot in Chile. My job is simple: let you focus entirely on the image while my team and I take care of everything else.

I understand the workflows, I speak the same technical language as your on-set team, and I anticipate issues before they arise. From the first technical brief to the final backup of the day, every detail is handled.

Chile is one of the world's most versatile production destinations. Within just a few hours, you can move from the driest desert on Earth to snow-capped mountains, from a modern metropolis to the Pacific coast, or to the untamed landscapes of Patagonia. The locations are extraordinary, and we know how to work in them.
